    Factions

    There are many so called 'Major Factions' which populate JRR Tolkins world of Lord of the Rings, many of which are not covered here. Rather than going over them all, the purpose of this website is to focus on the three major 'hero' races of Elves, Dwarfs, and Men.

    Hero Race

    A hero race is defined as a race which accompanies our main heros on their quest. A hero race which would fall under this category that are not covered here are Hobbits. If you are curious about where to find more information on hobbits, please feel free to follow the following link. It will take you to a page which goes into the race more indepth.

    Elves

    Tall, nimble, and fair, the elves of Middle Earth are found in most enchanted forests. One of the most benounced being the forest of Mirkwood. These Mirkwood elves are known for their expert use of the bow and arrow.

    Dwarfs

    Short, stout, and quick to anger, the dwarfs of Middle Earth are found in most mountain rangers which scatter themselves over the surface of Middle Earth. Under each of these mountains, a different faction of dwarf resides. The one thing every dwarf has in common with one another, if it isn't blood or faction, it is their greed for gold.

    Men

    Brave, strong willed, yet desire power more than all else, the Humans of Middle Earth can be found almost anywhere in Middle Earth. Though they mostly stay to their major boarders of Gondor or Rohan, they can be scattered about in almost any villiage or town one may visit. Because they are scattered so much without any true singular homeland, most humans have a different set of skills depending on where they were raised. For example, the humans of Rohan tend to be the best horse riders in all of Middle Earth where as the humans of Mirkwood tend to make very skilled rangers.
