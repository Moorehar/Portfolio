    Rivendell

    Rivendell is described as being one of the major footholds of elves in Middle Earth. It is here where Elrdon, a high authority in Elf Society, resides and where many major events occur within the story of Middle Earth. It is here where the plan for the destruction of The One Ring was produced and where the journey began for The Fellowship of the Ring.

    Mirkwood

    Mirkwood is a forest which holds a major trade route used by elves on pilgrimage. But as time passed, and dark forces began to twist the forest, the elves and men who called it home were mostly driven north to the mountains. Thranduil, The King of the Woodland Realm, was the leader at this time.
